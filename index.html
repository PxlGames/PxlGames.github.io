About me:

I am a game developer, an artist, a musician and a voice actor.

I have been working with a team called Team Melon, and we have published multiple different and unique games!

My games:

These images are screenshots from some of my games, which I have made and worked on.

Most of the games have been made during a game jam. Game jams are challenges where you have to make a game in a specified amount of time and with a specified theme.

I have the most experience in making 2D games, but I also can work in 3D. When it comes to art I prefer making pixel art.
