about me

I'm a game developer, who dabbles in multiple different sides of game development, these sides include programming, music composition & 2D and 3D-art.

I have extensive experience collaborating with diverse individuals and teams to create different projects. Among these, I am proud to be a continuing member of Team Melon, where I contribute as a versatile generalist.

My skill set includes proficiency in Unity for game development, Blender for 3D modeling and animation, and C# for programming. I am also adept at video editing with DaVinci Resolve and compose original music using FL Studio, allowing me to bring a well-rounded approach to creative and technical challenges.
