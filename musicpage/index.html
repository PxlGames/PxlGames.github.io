About my Music:

I like making music, most of the music I have ever made have been made in a music jam, a music jam is a challenge where you make music in a specified amount of time with a theme. Almost all of the music jams have had a time limit of less than 30-minutes.

Tools I use for composing:

I started composing music with a piano, then I moved to using a web based DAW called BeepBox, after using that I moved to a modified version of BeepBox, called JummBuss

Altough the sound and instrument quality are not the best, I find JummBuss easy to use, it is pattern based, and it has some options for easing the progress of composition. If you are interested,

jummbus.bitbucket.io
